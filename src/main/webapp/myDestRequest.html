Oops! Non-sufficient funds...

Prevention:
Carrying a higher balance in the checking account
- that is to always have a "buffer" amount just in case an unexpected check does clear
Better balancing techniques
Overdraft protection
- This may be in the form of a link to a savings account from which funds will be automatically transferred, a credit card, or, a line of credit designed specially for this purpose.
Using a credit/debit card or cash instead of checks.
